Fluffy Pancakes

  • Time: 20 minutes
  • Serves: 4 people
Ingredients Quantity
Flour 1 cup
Milk 1 cup
Egg 1
Sugar 2 tbsp
Baking powder 1 tsp

Step 1: Mix all dry ingredients in a bowl.

Step 2: Add milk and egg. Stir until smooth.

Step 3: Cook on a heated pan until golden brown on both sides.

Source: AllRecipes

Creamy Chicken Pasta

  • Time: 35 minutes
  • Serves: 3 people
Ingredients Quantity
Pasta 250 g
Chicken breast 1 piece
Cream 1 cup
Garlic 2 cloves
Parmesan 50 g

Step 1: Boil pasta according to package instructions.

Step 2: Cook chicken in a pan until golden.

Step 3: Add garlic and cream, simmer for 5 minutes.

Step 4: Mix with pasta and sprinkle parmesan on top.

Source: BBC Good Food
